# ProPublica and the Oregonian's "Nike Is Moving Jobs to Low-Wage Regions of Indonesia"

Published today, March 3: https://www.propublica.org/article/nike-jobs-indonesia-living-wages

This report is a great example of business accountability reporting. They used hard facts and data analysis, shown in visuals that readers can digest, to disprove PR-y statements made by a well-known and highly-supported company about their labor practices.

My only note is that the reporters should have referenced where they got the data briefly higher in the story. They have a methodology section at the bottom, which is great, but for much of the story the reader is asked to trust the data at face value. This, generally, isn't best practice.
